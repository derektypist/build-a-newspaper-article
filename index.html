8 January 2025

Additional Fixture at Chelmsford City Racecourse

BHA announce additional Saturday fixtures at Ffos Las and Chelmsford City Racecourse

By Peter Scargill

Additional Fixtures will be held on Saturday at Chelmsford and Ffos Las, where Wetherby's meeting was called off on Wednesday.

The extra racedays were announced on Wednesday by the BHA, with the authority also confirming that Newcastle's all-weather card will be brought forward to 1.50pm from its originally scheduled start of 1.50pm.
